Design Thinking Project: ADi

The ADi project focuses on improving the dining experience for UTM students and staff. It introduces a smart AI-based system that personalizes meal recommendations, tracks food history, and streamlines restaurant management. The app prototype addresses key challenges such as unclear working hours, lack of menus, and navigation difficulties, creating a seamless ecosystem for dining services.
